📖 Our History

Freedom Park Preschool & Nursery was established in 2016 to provide quality early childhood education and daycare in the Wadmukhwadi and Alandi areas of Pune. Since then, we have been a trusted partner for hundreds of families in our community.

🏛️ Independent Early Childhood Centre

Freedom Park Preschool & Nursery operates as an independent early childhood education and daycare center. It follows age-appropriate early learning practices designed to develop foundational skills and confidence in young children. While specific external board accreditation is not listed publicly, the programs align with recognised early childhood educational standards.

🤝 School Partnerships

Freedom Park is recognised as an Official Preschool Partner of Radcliffe School and holds a Certificate of Collaboration with MIT-World Peace School, Alandi — a testament to our commitment to quality education and community partnerships.
